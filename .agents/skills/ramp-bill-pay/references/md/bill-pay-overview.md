Source: https://support.ramp.com/hc/en-us/articles/4417743160211-Bill-Pay-Overview

## Overview

With Ramp, you can handle your company's spending in a single place. With Bill Pay, you can email or upload your invoices and create, approve, and pay your bills in under 60 seconds. We sync every bill and every payment to your accounting software. It's the fastest bill payment product on the market.

## [Bill payment methods](https://support.ramp.com/hc/en-us/articles/4417836454419-Bill-payment-methods)

Lightning-fast payments with multiple ways to pay. For each bill payment, Ramp initiates two payments. The first is to withdraw the funds (called a debit) from your selected bank account, and the second is to push the funds (called a credit) to your vendor using the payment details you provided. See how we pay your vendors on time.

## Tax support

Ramp Bill Pay has begun providing vendor tax support. Bill Pay users can add and store their vendors' W9 information directly in Ramp and export it for easier 1099 processing.

Please read more about Bill Pay Tax Support [here](https://support.ramp.com/hc/en-us/articles/11030575950739-Bill-Pay-Tax-Support).

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I use Ramp to issue invoices and collect payments from my customers?**

Bill Pay is Ramp's Accounts Payable (AP) product, designed for paying your vendors and managing outgoing payments. It does not support issuing invoices to your own customers or collecting incoming payments from them.
